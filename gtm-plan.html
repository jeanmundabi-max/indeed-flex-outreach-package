10-Day Launch Sequence

Day-by-day actions to activate the post-Coople window

Days 1 to 5 are internal setup. Days 6 to 10 are live outreach. All within the 6-week window before summer season closes the PSL.
Day Action Owner Output
Day 1 Account list segmentation — sort the 30 Tier 1 accounts by urgency score. Highest urgency = Coople ex-clients with summer season dependency and no confirmed replacement. Research MDB Prioritised outreach order. Top 10 first wave accounts confirmed.
Day 2 Decision-maker verification — confirm current role for each Tier 1 contact. Check LinkedIn for recent activity. Flag any stale contacts for replacement. Research MDB Verified contact list for Days 6 to 10. Zero stale names.
Day 3 Email personalisation pass — customise the Trigger email subject line and Layer 3 scene paragraph for the 10 priority accounts. Each opener references something specific about that company. Copywriting MDB 10 personalised email versions. Review by Sarah before sending.
Day 4 LinkedIn profile audit — confirm Sarah's LinkedIn profile positioning for enterprise buyers. Connection request template prepared for each of the 10 priority contacts. LinkedIn MDB + Sarah LinkedIn sequence ready. Profile reviewed and optimised if needed.
Day 5 SmartLead setup and review — email sequences loaded into sending tool. Sarah reviews all 10 personalised emails. Approval gate before first send. Email MDB + Sarah Sarah confirms sends. All sequences approved. Zero unknown errors.
Day 6 First wave sends — Trigger email to 10 priority Tier 1 accounts. Subject: "Coople's clients." LinkedIn connection requests sent in parallel. Email LinkedIn MDB (sends) 10 emails delivered. 10 LinkedIn connection requests sent.
Day 7 Monitor and flag — track opens and replies from Day 6 sends. Flag any out-of-office replies for timing adjustment. First LinkedIn connection acceptances likely. Monitor MDB Initial reply data. First connection requests accepted.
Day 8 Second wave sends — Trigger email to next 10 Tier 1 accounts. LinkedIn connection requests for second batch. LinkedIn follow-up message to Day 6 connection acceptances. Email LinkedIn MDB 20 emails total sent. LinkedIn sequence progressing on Day 6 batch.
Day 9 Reply qualification — assess all replies from Days 6 to 8. Genuine PSL evaluation vs polite brush-off. Route high-urgency replies to Sarah immediately with context. Qualification MDB Qualified reply log. Sarah briefed on any genuinely interested prospects.
Day 10 Week 1 reporting — accounts reached, open rates, reply rates, qualified conversations. Sarah reviews data and approves continuation. Friction email sequence prepared for Day 14 follow-up. Reporting MDB + Sarah Week 1 report. Sarah decision: continue, adjust, or pause for Loom video addition.
