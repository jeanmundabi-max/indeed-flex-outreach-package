Evaluation
How enterprise buyers assess and shortlist Flex Plus
 Enterprise procurement teams who have found Indeed Flex run a standard evaluation. Demo, reference check, pricing discussion. The Flex Plus product wins on merits when it gets in front of the right buyer. Challenge: how few enterprise buyers get to this stage through a proactive channel. Most evaluations are inbound-initiated. Pre-qualification of every inbound and outbound lead by buying urgency. Research brief on each enterprise account before the first call — trigger event, decision-maker background, informal shortlist status, recommended approach. Sales team enters every evaluation informed, not cold.

Each loop self-reinforces. The first win in each loop makes the second win easier. After 12 months, all three loops are running simultaneously and the cost per new enterprise account falls every quarter.

Each Flex Plus win in a sector creates case study proof that accelerates the next win in the same sector. The first contract catering win makes the second contract catering evaluation faster — the prospect already knows a peer who uses Flex Plus.
