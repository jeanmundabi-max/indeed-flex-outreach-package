Where enterprise Flex Plus revenue is leaking

🔴
LEAK 1: Stage 1 visibility — Critical

Enterprise procurement teams form informal shortlists before the formal brief goes out. Indeed Flex is not in those conversations. By the time a formal brief arrives, the preferred vendor is already identified. Stage 1 is invisible to inbound-only suppliers.

🔴
LEAK 2: Post-Coople window timing — Critical

Coople exited the UK market on 1 January 2026. Summer season starts in 6 weeks. Companies that have not yet signed a replacement preferred supplier agreement are in live evaluation right now. This window closes and does not reopen until autumn.

🟡
LEAK 3: Authority gap in enterprise discovery — Important

Enterprise HR Directors searching "contingent workforce platform UK" find the Indeed brand, not Flex Plus case studies or VMS comparison content. The authority gap means Instawork and StaffHeroes are winning the discovery stage in searches Indeed Flex should own.

🟡
LEAK 4: US new market penetration — Important

8 new US markets launched December 2025. Five months in, no confirmed systematic outreach to enterprise accounts in Charlotte, Phoenix, Las Vegas, or Orlando. The first 6 months is the peak window for enterprise account formation. That window is closing.

🟢
LEAK 5: Marketplace to Flex Plus upgrade path — Monitor

Existing Marketplace accounts are not being systematically upgraded to Flex Plus VMS. The conversation from "we use your app for shifts" to "we want your VMS across all our sites" requires proactive account development, not just inbound upgrade requests.
